Introduction

Not long after, the queen gave birth to a little girl who was exactly as she had wished—her skin was as white as snow, her lips as red as blood, and her hair as black as ebony. She named her Snow White. Sadly, the queen died soon after the child was born.

A year later, the king married again. His new wife was very beautiful but also proud and vain. She could not tolerate anyone being more beautiful than she was. She owned a magical mirror, and every day she would ask it:
"Mirror, mirror on the wall, who is the fairest of them all?"
The mirror always answered:
"You, my queen, are the fairest of all."

Roles and Responsibilities

The queen was content because she knew the mirror only spoke the truth. But Snow White grew up, and with each passing year, she became more beautiful. By the time she was seven, she was even more beautiful than the queen herself.
